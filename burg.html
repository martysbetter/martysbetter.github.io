Intrusion Protection

Your home or business is one of the most important investments you will make in your lifetime, protect it.

Specializing in residential and commercial burglar alarm systems, Advanced Security Systems can design a custom system to protect your property from intrusion. Utilizing the latest technology, we make it easy to secure your property and stay connected.

Hardwired & Wireless Devices Available

Most devices available in both hardwired and wireless versions. Upgrading your system or adding additional devices has never been easier. No longer is it necessary to run wires or drill holes.

No Phone Line Needed!

Some alarm systems fail because they depend on landline phone to transmit an alarm signal. 100% wireless radio communication ensures your system will be able to communicate your alarm condition.

24-Hour Central Station Monitoring

The moments between the start of a fire and the arrival of first responders can make all the difference in the world. Our UL listed central station is ready to process your alarm signals quickly and efficiently to dispatch the proper authorities to your location.
