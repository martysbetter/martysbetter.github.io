What kind of devices can be installed on my alarm system?

There are many types of burglar, fire, and environmental alarm devices available.

Most available both hard-wired and wireless:

  • Window Contacts, Door Contacts, Motion Detectors, Glass Break Sensors, Shock Sensors
  • Smoke Detectors, Heat Detectors, Carbon Monoxide Detectors
  • Flood Sensors, Low Temperature Sensors, Natural Gas Detectors
  • Wireless Radio Communication - No Phone Lines Needed!

Do you offer any free alarm systems?

Regardless of what some companies may tell you, there is no such thing as a truly free alarm system. The companies that typically offer these free systems, usually include a minimal amount of low-end devices or components (leaving areas of the home unprotected), while the actual cost of the 'free' system is included in the inflated monthly monitoring charge. Combined with what is often a 5+ year contract, these 'free' systems are simply paid for over time, by the subscriber.

Advanced Security Systems does not believe in misleading advertising or deceptive promotions. While there may be companies out there who are cheaper than us, we have all the confidence in the world that you will find a much higher value in our products and services.

What is a 'Zone Fault' ? Why does my keypad say 'Not Ready' ?

Your system can only be armed if and when all zones are seen as closed by the alarm control panel. This means that before your system will allow you to arm it, you will need to make sure all doors/windows are closed and that no one is in range of any motion detectors. If the system sees that a door/window is open, or if it picks up motion from any of the motion detectors, you will not be able to arm the system until the zone is closed.

Please refer to our Panel Manuals page to see how to check which zone is faulted on your particular system.
