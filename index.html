Medical Alarm

Medical pendants can save lives by notifying the medical authorities to an emergency when it is not possible to get to a phone. Worn as a necklace or watch, a wireless medical pendant will provide the assitance needed at the press of a button.

Environmental Alarms

Protect your home from the elements and possible costly damage. Flood/water sensors and low temperature sensors can help mitigate damage by alerting you to the presence of water or a drop in temperature.

Panic Alarm

Stationary or wireless panic alarms provide immediate police dispatch in the event a dangerous situation arises. Configured to be an audible or silent alarm, panic alarms can be worn on ones person or fixed to a location.
